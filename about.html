About us

Shree Ashirwad Pharma was estabilished in 2017 and started as a chemical produt manufacturing company supplying industrial adhesives including for medical use. Especially, manufacturing synthetic orthopedic casting tape.

With consistent and passionate R&D investment, we have been keeping our product's superb quality, which gave us high reputation from our valuable customers.
